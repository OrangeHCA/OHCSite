Who We Are

We are The Orange Head Clan, the most serious unserious group there is. Originally founded in 2017 by Kraevous (Sir Orange Head) on Minecraft Xbox 360 Edition and Clash of Clans, it was revived in 2021 by both Kraevous and XxJVG1xX (OrangeGaming4K) to now have clans on many games, including Roblox, Minecraft, Clash of Clans, GTA and Call of Duty being our mainstream so far.

Our aim is to be one of the most powerful civilisations in the gaming world while also being the one place where people can have a laugh and not feel any sort of need to be formal. We got goals and ambition but also a laid back nature. If you want a pressure free environment but also want to further your skills within gaming, join us and become apart of the family, it's only ever growing.

Most importantly, make sure to join the communications server to connect with all of the other games and meeting new people within our family. See you there!
